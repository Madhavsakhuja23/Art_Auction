Legacy Classics

Legacy Classics is a landmark artwork, celebrated for its masterful technique and evocative portrayal of a pivotal era in art history. Appearing for the first time at auction in over two decades, the piece drew considerable attention from collectors globally.

Artist: Henri Dubois

Medium: Oil on canvas

Dimensions: 110 x 85 cm (43.3 × 33.5 in)

Year: 1923

Description: This historic masterpiece captures the tranquil essence of early 20th-century French landscapes, blending rich color fields with a subtle impressionist touch.

Auction Date: July 1, 2025

Estimate Price: $15,000 – $25,000
